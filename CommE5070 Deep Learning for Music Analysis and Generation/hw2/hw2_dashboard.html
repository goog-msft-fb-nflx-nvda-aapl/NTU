Methodology NotesCondition Strategy
Text Condition (Medium)
Caption from Qwen2-Audio-7B-Instruct passed directly as MusicGen text prompt. Covers genre, tempo, mood, instruments.
Melody Condition (Medium)
Chromagram extracted from target audio via MusicGen's built-in chroma pipeline. MIR-based, rule-compliant (no raw audio conditioning).
CFG Scale (Strong)
Classifier-free guidance set to 3.0 globally. Per-track tuning is a TODO improvement.
What we did NOT do
No audio conditioning directly. No autoencoder encode/decode of target. No model training or fine-tuning.
